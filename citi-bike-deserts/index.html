Citi Bike or City Hike? Mapping NYC's Most Unreliable Stations

Tracking when and where users may be most likely to encounter ununsable stations.

By C.J. Robinson

Citi Bike's website claims to allow you to "Bike NYC. Like, all of it." For many New Yorkers, however, a recent analysis found that when and where you bike could preclude your ability from conviently docking in certain neighborhoods.
